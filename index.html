FAQ
Can I choose the voice actor for my content?
Yes, you can choose from a range of voices with different styles, tones, and accents. We can also use audio samples to clone voices (with consent) to make it sound like a specific person. Just let us know your preferences when you submit your content.
Can you customize the format of the conversation or style of the speakers?
Yes! We'll work with you to match the conversation format and speaking style to your brand's voice.
What is included in the podcast-style audio file?
Our podcast-style episodes include a professional-grade conversation by two of our custom, high-quality voices. We deliver a finished mp3 file to you, ready to be published on platforms of your choice.
Can I edit the script?
Yes, we include one script revision per episode, or more revisions for an additional fee.
What content formats do you accept?
We can accept most written content formats such as articles, blog posts, research papers, or plain text.
Is there a limit to the length of the original content?
There is no limit but we recommend keeping the original content under 5,000 words for best results, but we can work with longer pieces as well.
How much does it cost?
It all depends on the length and complexity of your content, as well as the number of files you need. Get in touch to discuss your budget.
How long does it take to produce an episode?
We turnaround an episode in 1 business day to allow time for quality control. If you would like revisions, especially for complex or longer content, more time may be needed.
Can I use the audio for commercial purposes?
Yes, you can use the audio for commercial purposes such as advertising, marketing, or training. However, please note that you may need to obtain licensing or permissions for any copyrighted materials included in your content.
What languages do you offer for the voiceover narration?
Currently, we offer our highest quality voices in English. We plan to offer additional languages such as Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese. Please let us know if you're looking for a specific language, and we can work with you to see if it's possible!
Do you offer a free trial or sample?
Yes! We want you to see for yourself how it works. Reach out and we'll work out the details.
